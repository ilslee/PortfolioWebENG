Benzamin AI Mattress M1

Mar,2022-Jun,2024

Benzamin is an AI mattress designed to improve sleep quality. It tracks the user's sleep in real time and delivers vibration feedback to enhance recovery during sleep. Additionally, it provides sleep coaching services based on accumulated sleep data to improve sleep quality.

Harmony of
functionality and design

To emphasize its identity as a sleep improvement device and showcase the pioneering spirit of a new player, I aimed for a design that stands apart from traditional mattresses as furniture. the mattress designed to seamlessly integrate functional elements requiring explanation, highlighting them without making them feel out of place.

Balance of
performance and comfort

Benzamin's main devices and sensors are embedded within the mattress. To ensure device stability and comfortable usability, we utilized four specially designed foam layers. A unique structure was designed to allow the sensors to measure bio-data from within the mattress.

Visual Communication

A number of partner companies participated in the development and production of the mattress. As the product designer and production manager, I gathered input from experts in various fields and coordinated to align with the intended purpose of the design. Throughout this process, I utilized various visual materials to prevent misunderstandings during production and to effectively exchange necessary information.

DayNa : Companion App

Mar,2022-Dec,2023

It is a companion app designed to pair with the Benzamin mattress. The app allows users to control the device, set up Wi-Fi and Bluetooth environments, and manage user profiles. Convenience features such as alarms and real-time bio-data monitoring are available. Additionally, the app visualizes accumulated sleep data in various formats for the user.

Benzamin M

Mar,2023-Sep,2024

A medical device incorporating Benzamin AI mattress technology. Although it does not provide the sleep improvement effects of vibration feedback, its compact design enables long-term bio-monitoring. It was developed under stricter standards to meet medical device certifications. With the addition of accessories, this adaptable product can be applied to a variety of mattresses instantly.

Flexible Design and Structure

Benzamin M is designed to be compatible with bedding, inside of mattresses, standard beds, and hospital beds. To achieve this, a modular mechanism was adopted, and each component was designed to be adaptable or replaceable based on its intended purpose.

Medical Device Safety
Certification Achieved

The hardware was kept streamlined while meeting medical device safety standards. In particular, meticulous design was required to satisfy dustproof and waterproof requirements. I actively participated in this process, ensuring both aesthetics and usability were achieved simultaneously.

Active Adoption of
New Hardware

Identified and adopted new hardware essential for the design. Proposed and developed new sensor designs for integration into Benzamin M, and proactively led the selection process for Pogo connectors to implement connection components.
